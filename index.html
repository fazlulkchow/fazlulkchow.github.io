Journey

Fazlul leads Data Analytics & Efficient Frontiers at Innovision Consulting. He spearheads market research for private sector, start-ups, investors, driving strategic initiatives in innovative solutions and the digital economy.

As Certified Financial Modeling and Valuation Analyst, Fazlul's dedication to excellence evident in passing CFA Level 1 in 2016. BBA and MBA from North South University reinforce his expertise in management consulting, providing exceptional insights for strategic decision-making.

Prior to joining Innovision, Fazlul excelled at Confidence Group (a local conglomerate in the infrastructure sector) in its "Investment and Future Business Department" for Capital Investments, Corporate Financing, Financial Planning & Analysis, and Project Financing. Fazlul analyzed and appraised all aspects of new investment proposals (debt and or equity) including financial, operational, commercial, legal, corporate governance, and environmental and social sustainability elements; and prepares investment recommendations where appropriate. Involved in in-depth investment analysis of ~BDT 1 billion to 40 billion infrastructural projects for market capitalization enhancement.

Publicly declared developments:

  • 660MW gas-based power plant in Mirsarai Economic Zone ($550mn investment & 2nd largest private power plant)
  • Geotextile manufacturer (BDT 200cr investment with production ~80 ton/day, ~500 employees)
  • ATC acquired 51% of KTBL for ~12,000 mobile towers plan (KTBL's $1bn investment in wireless tower infrastructure)
  • Confidence Cement Dhaka Ltd.(BDT 815cr worthy expensionary investment with 18lac ton/year production capacity)

Previously, as a Senior Associate at Inspira Advisory and Consulting Ltd., Fazlul advised private and development sector clients through data-driven research, macroeconomic analysis, risk assessment, value chain analysis, and more.

Commenced career as Research Analyst at The Governance & Justice Group , pivotal role in national pro ject "Justice Audit Bangladesh," reflecting dedication to meaningful impact. Fazlul's prowess as a business strategist is underscored by collaborations with esteemed organizations such as Oxfam, Sesame Workshop, GIZ Bangladesh, USAID, UKAID, BBS, Supreme Court of Bangladesh, and several ministries of Bangladesh.

Services

  • Strategic Planning

    Comprehensive roadmap to outline the goals, strategies, and actions needed to achieve the desired results for a new business which includes market research, demand forecasting, business analytics and identifying potential risks and opportunities.
    A well-crafted business plan as well as investment pitch-deck is essential for securing funding, attracting investors, and guiding the overall direction of a company.

  • Financial Planning & Analysis

    Creating financial plan for a business including forecasting future financial performance, analyzing current financial data, and identifying potential financial risks and opportunities through financial modeling and budgeting techniques.
    Making strategic decisions about investments, capital expenditures, and other financial matters like improving profitability and efficiency for ensuring the long-term financial health and stability of a business.

  • Investment Research

    Gathering and analyzing information about potential investments in order to make informed decisions. This includes researching companies, industries, and markets, as well as evaluating the potential risks and returns of different investment options.
